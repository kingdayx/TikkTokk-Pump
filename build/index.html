    What is our long-term plan?

    Our mission is to create a scalable, reliable and efficient infrastructure for the purpose of the monetization of TikTokâ„¢ content creators and the yielding of consistent high returns for our investors. We strive to achieve this objective through the power of decentralized technologies.

    Why did we implement the BEP-20 network?

    After extensive consideration, we decided to opt for the BEP-20 network instead of any of the other alternatives for a multitude of reasons. The primary ones being: the low and stable transaction fees. This ensures that when our token changes hands our community only has to pay a near-negligible cost. The BEP-20 network also has exceptionally fast block confirmation times, ensuring that our most precious assettime is never wasted. Moreover, the network offers smart contracts, a feature we intend to use to a great degree in the near future.

    How does token burning work?

    We burn 5% of our token on a monthly basis. This means that 5% of our token is permanently removed from the circulating supply by sending it to the 0x0 address. For this purpose we decided to not automate this purpose and rather do this manually. This is set up this way in order to make ourselves adaptable and possibly increase or decrease this rate in the future.

    Why burn tokens in the first place?

    Burning leads to the deflation of a token. Due to the law of supply and demand this constantly decreasing supply ensures a stable and constant increase in price with an exponential impact. In essence- the more tokens are burnt, the more the price increases. Consequently this mechanism yields extremely high returns to our investors.
